Resturants

Kuwait is known to have some of the most unique and authentic resturants ranging from different cuisines. All the details are carefully thought out from the moment you are seated until you step foot out, it's all about the experience.

-Dar Hamad: If you want to try authentic Kuwaiti food then this place is highly recommeneded, aside from the food the interior design is gorgeous.

-Maki: Maki is a Japanese resturant and it has a few locations across the city. It's dishes are authentic and there sushi selection is immaculate.

-Burger Boutique: Burger Boutique also known as B+F is a local burger resturant, they have many unique burger selections.

-Melenzane: Melenzane is an Italian resturant but with a twist, they have incredible Italian food that is sure to make you happy.
